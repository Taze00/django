ABOUT ME PLAYLIST

Track 1: "Roots and Routes"

Born in Berlin, but now calling Werder home – a slight twist in the journey, yet my love for the capital remains untouched.

Track 2: "Digital Vibes"

My days are spent diving into the world of marketing and web design, thanks to my studies in Business Informatics. It's not just about the tech for me; it's about creating connections that count.

Track 3: "Berlin Beat Hopper"

When I'm in Berlin, I love to explore the club scene. Club hopping is my thing; a different club every weekend. Tresor gets a shoutout, not because it's the only place I go, but because when I do, it's always a blast. Yet, it's the city's diversity that truly captivates me.

Track 4: "Life on the Pitch"

Twenty years on the field, and still no desire to hang up my boots. Soccer is more than a game; it's one of those constants that keep me grounded.

Track 5: "Echoes of Me"

Talking big about myself isn't my style. I much prefer to let the things I do and my passions speak for themselves. Through this playlist, you, the listener, can catch the vibe of my world.

Track 6: "Window to My World"

This website is like a set of tracks, each offering a glimpse into what drives me – from Berlin's endless club landscape to a peaceful day on the soccer field. Not overloaded, just an honest insight.
